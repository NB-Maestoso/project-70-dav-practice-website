    M.L. Khanna DAV Public School, Dwarka believes in the culmination of knowledge and talent into performance. Working under the aegis of DAV College Managing Committee, Chitra Gupta Road, New Delhi, the school has perpetuated a conducive environment setting benchmarks in varied realms of educational arena. Since its inception in 1990, the school has adopted a holistic approach through a well thought curriculum including modern teaching methods interwoven with rich vedic culture to sustain high degree of ethics and morality among students. The school is privileged to have a dynamic state of the art complex on a lush green four acre campus which serves as an ultimate habitat for learning. Today, the school is replete with the strength of over 3000 students and the work force of over 125 highly proficient and experienced faculty members focusing on success under the able guidance of the Principal and the DAV College Managing Committee. Principal Ms. Monika Mehan, who strongly believes that education is the connecting force between what our society needs and what it should be, has initiated a number of programmes in school like Generation Global – An innovative school‘s programme by the Tony Blair Faith Foundation and Erudition -Distinguished Guest Lecture Series that offers a robust slate of lecture and enriches the intellectual scientific and cultural life of students and faculty of DAV Dwarka by inviting distinguished people from varied fields to the school campus. A wide range of activities conducted through 21 activity clubs in school - Face to Faith Club, Interact Club, Heritage Club, Health and Wellness Club, Culinary Club, Disaster Management Club, Photography Club, Dramatics Club, Bonsai Club, Music Club, Art Club etc - play an important role in promoting varied interests of students, embedding global dimension in their outlook and help them acquire an international and digital competence. Perpetual encouragement, inspiration and guidance from the enlightened parents combined with the efforts of a qualified, dedicated and tech-savvy team of teachers, enable the students and the school to achieve success in educational endeavours.
